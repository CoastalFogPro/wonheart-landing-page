Original Music with an Infectious Groove

Joelyn Lutz is a singer/songwriter and performer on the beautiful central coast of California. With over four decades of musical enterprising, ranging from lead guitarist in an all-girl rock band to teaching vocals and guitar, Joelyn has plugged into the music world in every imaginable variation. Performing for spiritual centers up and down the central coast and playing in bands spanning every genre, Joelyn's music embodies a rich fusion of rock, country, jazz, rhythm and blues and latin character.

About Joelyn

My music has changed through the years and I wouldn't have it any other way. It started in the 80's with an all-female band called Teazer where we played pop & rock with the typical messages about the good and bad of love relationships. Over the years my life has changed and I have become more conscious regarding my music. The albums Just Breathe, A Matter of Zen and my latest release All Of Us are works purposely to remind us just how incredible we are and that we matter in this world. From ballads to an infectious groove to move you in your body I'm also devoted to the tuning of 432 HZ (which is known as the healing vibration). While listening to my music I hope it reminds you that we're all LOVE and that what we say and do really matters to create more.
